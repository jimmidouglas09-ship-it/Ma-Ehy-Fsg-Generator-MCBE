Bedrock 1.16 FSG Structureless Generator

Help

This generator will guarantee very good seeds to practice. There are multiple filters:
Stronghold Village: guaranteed stronghold village and fastion in the nether.
Stronghold Village (packless): same as stronghold village, but doesn't require a pack (and therefore has less seeds).
Ruined Portal: guarantees a very close ruined portal with looting 3 sword, 27 iron nuggets, a flint and steel, 8-9 obsidian and a golden axe and pickaxe (with the pack). There is fastion in nether, and the strongholds are guaranteed to be within 2000 blocks from 0 0, which means that educated travel is guaranteed to be good.
Village: guarantees a village (not stronghold) + 10 lava blocks rp or mega canyon, fastion in nether, sh within 2k of 0 0.
Classic: guarantees stronghold village and insta fort, greatly increased pearl rates for classic.
Structureless: guarantees mega canyon at spawn for enter and iron ore, fastion in nether, sh within 2k form 0 0.
For any additional info, request and feedback join the discord. Coded by ma_ehy

Ranked Help

Ranked seeds are like fsg, but filtered to be even better in quality and stricter in where the structures generates. The addon will also grant extremely enhanced rng, and - for the ruined portal behavior pack within the addon - will force certain structures to always generate at specific locations. For more information about ranked and the addon, join the discord server.
